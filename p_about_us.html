Under new management, Elwood Hobbies can help you with your remote controlled vehicles. We sell model cars, boats and aircraft and will repair everything we sell. We are known as "The Most Complete Hobby Shop on Long Island."

- Ralph Tedeschi

At Elwood Hobbies, we are more than just a hobby shop; we are a passionate community of enthusiasts dedicated to bringing the joy of hobbies to life. With a history steeped in a love for creativity, craftsmanship, and playfulness, our journey began as a small family-owned business. Over the years, we've evolved into a haven for hobbyists of all ages, where novices and experts alike come together to explore, create, and share their passions. Our dedicated team is driven by a shared commitment to providing you with the highest quality products, expert advice, and a welcoming environment where every customer feels valued and inspired. From model trains to R/C products, art supplies to collectibles, Elwood Hobbies is your trusted partner in nurturing your hobbies and making every moment a masterpiece. Join us in celebrating the essence of hobbies and craftsmanship. Together, we build dreams, one hobby at a time.
